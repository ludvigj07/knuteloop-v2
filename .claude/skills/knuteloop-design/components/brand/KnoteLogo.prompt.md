The Knuteloop brand lockup — the white/blue loop-knot mark plus the "Knuteloop" wordmark in Bricolage Grotesque; use in headers, splash, and onboarding.

```jsx
<KnoteLogo size={28} />
<KnoteLogo variant="mark" tone="primary" size={32} />
<KnoteLogo tone="inverse" />   {/* on navy / photo */}
```

`variant`: `full` (default) or `mark` (loop only). `tone`: `ink`, `primary`, `inverse`. Keep clear space around it; never recolor the wordmark outside these tones.
